Red high heeled shoe

You can get a platform at the front of a shoe, with a chunky heel at the back, or a platform at the front which allows a stiletto heel to be higher.

A platform on a stiletto allows more comfort for the wearer and makes the shoe easier to walk in. The platform shoe is associated with 70’s footwear fashion but in the 2019’s a platform on the front of a shoe made a resurgence.
