WOMEN SUNGLASSES

Round

John Lennon always comes to mind with this iconic style.

While the lenses are always round, the frames can be small or large, and metal or plastic.

Throw on a pair of these frames and sway to the music!

Cat Eye

Categorized by upswept angles and retro frames.

Cat eye sunglasses were originally made famous by stars like Marilyn and Audrey.

With feminine vibes and feline prints,retro lovers should snag this style right.


Keyhole Bridge


Keyhole bridges add a subtle vintage feel to any pair of sunglasses.

Keyhole bridges got their name because the opening at the bridge looks a keyhole!

This type of bridge is perfect for those who have a low nose bridge and need their shades to stay put.

They look great on everyone though.
